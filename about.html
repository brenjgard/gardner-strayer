About

A local agency focused on useful advice and long-term relationships.

Gardner & Strayer is built around the kind of service clients expect from a local independent agency: straightforward guidance, responsive follow-through, and coverage conversations that stay grounded in real life.

What working with our agency should feel like.

We help individuals, families, farms, and businesses review coverage with a practical mindset. That means understanding the property, vehicles, operations, and responsibilities you actually need to protect, then helping you compare options clearly.

Independent guidance also means the relationship does not stop once a policy is written. When something changes, when you want to revisit limits, or when a claim creates questions, you should still have a local agency to call.

What matters to us.

Local Perspective

We serve the homes, farms, drivers, and businesses that shape communities across North Central Ohio.

Independent Guidance

We help clients weigh options across multiple carriers instead of pushing a one-size-fits-all answer.

Long-Term Service

We want to be the agency clients keep calling as coverage needs change over time.
