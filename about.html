About

Independent insurance guidance with a local, long-term approach.

Gardner & Strayer serves individuals, families, farms, and businesses across North Central Ohio with practical advice, personal service, and relationships built to last.

An agency focused on clear advice and dependable follow-through.

As an independent agency, Gardner & Strayer helps clients review options carefully instead of forcing a one-size-fits-all answer. We take time to understand the property, vehicles, operations, and responsibilities that matter most to you.

That approach matters because insurance decisions are rarely one-time decisions. Policies change, businesses grow, family needs shift, and coverage questions come up. Our role is to stay useful before and after the policy is written.

What clients should expect from us.

Local Service

We understand the communities, homes, farms, and businesses we serve across North Central Ohio.

Independent Recommendations

We help clients compare coverage with a practical focus on protection, fit, and long-term value.

Long-Term Relationships

We aim to be the agency clients continue calling when coverage needs change or questions come up.
